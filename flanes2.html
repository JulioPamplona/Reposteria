Flan Napolitano


Back to menu



INGREDIENTS:

* 3/4 Cup Refined sugar
* 1 LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk Can
* 1 Package Cheese cream at room temperature (190 g)
* 5 Pieces Egg
* 1 Tablespoon Vanilla Essence
* 1 CARNATION® CLAVEL® Evaporated Milk Tin



Preparation:

1. Pour the sugar in a flanera and heat over medium heat to form the caramel; Carefully tilt the mold to cover the surface and walls.
2. Blend LA LECHERA® Condensed Milk with CARNATION® CLAVEL® Evaporated Milk, cream cheese, eggs and vanilla essence. Pour the preparation in the flanera and cover with aluminum foil sealing the edges. Place in a bowl and cook in a bain-marie in the oven at 180 ° C for 1 1/2 hours.
3. Remove from heat and let cool completely; Unmold and serve.
If you want you can add a little cocoa.
Remember that desserts can be included in the correct diet, as long as they are consumed moderately.
