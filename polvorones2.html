Polvorones


Back to menu



INGREDIENTS:

* 300 grams of vegetable shortening
* 1 1/2 cups of sugar
* 1 piece of egg
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 500 grams of flour
* 1/2 cup of sugar to decorate



Preparation:

1. Preheat the oven to 160 Â° C.
2. Accrete the butter with the sugar until it is incorporated. Add the egg and vanilla.
3. Add the flour little by little and beat until incorporated.

4. Place the dough on non-stick plastic and form a roll. Freeze about 30 minutes. Cut the polvorones approximately 2 cm thick and sprinkle with sugar. Place in a baking tray.
5. Bake 15 to 20 minutes or until the edges are golden brown. Chill a little and wallow in sugar while still hot.
